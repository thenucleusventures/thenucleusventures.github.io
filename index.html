Program journey
Navigating the early stages of a startups journey is one of the biggest and most enduring challenges any founder will face. From developing their idea and getting it all the way to market, the path is full of obstacles at every turn. The Nucleus has been developed to simplify the early stage journey, keeping entrepreneurs focused on the right actions at the right time. Each startup on the program embarks on a unique, structured path tailored to their individual needs and progress to date.
After an initial assessment of the business or idea, your program will commence focusing on one of three key stages:

Ideation

If you have an idea but have yet to act on it or you have started a business but have yet to validate your solution with the market, we’ll help you get your business off the ground and test your solution with prospective customers. Startups joining this stage of the program will benefit from:

  • Early market feedback gained from concept pitching to potential customers.
  • Bespoke incubation to support every aspect of getting their business off the ground.
  • Mentoring from experts matched to their unique needs, including a dedicated lead mentor
  • Early funding to develop their solution once market validation has been demonstrated (Selected companies only)

Execution

If you have already been able to demonstrate some degree of customer traction or product market fit, we’ll help you accelerate progress with structured coaching to develop your product and business overall and matchmaking with relevant industry partners and mentors. Startups joining this stage of the program will benefit from:

  • Mentoring from experts matched to their unique needs, including a dedicated lead mentor
  • Dealmaking support from a dedicated POC who will facilitate initial introductions to key industry, commercial, delivery and investment partners based on their needs
  • Rapid execution program comprising 12 weeks of intensive, structured development focusing on all key aspects of getting their business adopted by early customers.
  • Early funding to develop their solution once market validation has been demonstrated (Selected companies only)

Seed investment

If your startup is already enjoying early market traction and generating some revenues, we’ll provide you with bespoke support to help you launch a full go to market strategy. Startups joining at this stage of the program will benefit from:

  • Mentoring from experts matched to their unique needs, including a dedicated lead mentor
  • Dealmaking support from a dedicated dealmaker who will facilitate business expansion with introductions to key industry, commercial, delivery and investment partners.
  • Seed investment and investment support offered to a limited number of startups on either a standalone or co-investment basis, in addition to investment sourcing.
Why you should apply to the Nucleus
Support that you need
No two startups are the same and one size definitely does not fit all when it comes to startup support. Every startup on our program receives a tailored action plan at the outset based on their unique needs.
Market validation
Until a startup has real feedback from prospective customers, it’s basically operating wearing a blindfold and hoping for the best. We help to secure this critical validation early on to ensure the right focus from the get go.
Building for beyond
Long-term, sustainable success requires startups to think beyond immediate markets. Our founders build for scale from the start with access to a global community of advisors, investors and customers.
The network effect
No founder can go it alone. Access to knowledge, customers, investors advice and some good old moral support are essential. Enjoy access to a ready network of support, expertise and business prospects.
Milestone funding
Raising capital is a challenging and time-consuming process, especially in the early days. We break down and offer funding in line with key early stage milestones to ensure progress doesn’t get halted at critical moments.
Staying on track
New founders are pulled in multiple directions with limited resources and time. Our platform sets clear tasks alongside a dedicated mentor to help them prioritise and keep focused on what matters the most.
